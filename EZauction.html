Our Services

Live Auction


Ezauction offers livestock auctioneers and their bidders the web resources necessary to conduct profitable livestock online auctions. Livestock will be auctioned in live viewing, and bids may be placed from any location in Bataan.

Bidding


Ezauction enables anyone to bid on livestock purchases. It is a competitive procedure in which two or more parties compete with one another by increasing the price they are willing to pay to acquire the livestock.

Billing & Payment


Ezauction provides electronic bank payment and billiing services to individuals for bidding transactions with deducted commission and fees.
